About FD Calculator

What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a financial instrument provided by banks or NBFCs which provides investors a higher rate of interest than a regular savings account, until the given maturity date. It may or may not require the creation of a separate account.

How Our Calculator Works

Our FD Calculator helps you calculate the maturity amount and interest earned on your fixed deposit investment. The calculator uses the compound interest formula:

A = P * (1 + r/n)^(n*t)

Where:

  • A = Maturity Amount
  • P = Principal Amount
  • r = Annual Interest Rate
  • n = Compounding Frequency
  • t = Time in Years

Features

  • Calculate maturity amount for any principal amount
  • Support for different compounding frequencies (Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, Yearly)
  • Detailed breakdown of interest earned
  • User-friendly interface
  • Responsive design for all devices
